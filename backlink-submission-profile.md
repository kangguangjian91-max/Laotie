## Description (long, 500 chars)
Henan Laotie Steel Structure Engineering Co., Ltd. is a premier steel structure manufacturer based in Shangqiu, Henan, China. Since 2009, we have specialized in the design, fabrication, and export of custom steel structures including portal frame buildings, warehouses, factories, workshops, space frame trusses, floor deck systems, C&Z purlins, insulated sandwich panels, and prefab container houses. With 5 advanced production lines and a monthly capacity of 5,000 tons, we serve clients in 30+ countries across Southeast Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and Oceania. Our CE (EN 1090) and ISO 9001 certifications, combined with AWS D1.1 compliant welding and third-party inspection (SGS, BV), ensure consistent quality for every project.
