#5 — Steel Structure Maintenance Tips
标题 Steel Structure Maintenance: Essential Tips for Long-Lasting Buildings
内容 Proper maintenance extends the service life of steel buildings to 50+ years. Here's what every owner should know. Regular Inspection Schedule Every 6 months: - Check for rust spots, especially at joints - Inspect roof for leaks or damaged panels - Verify bolt tightness on major connections Every 12 months: - Inspect coating condition (paint or galvanization) - Check drainage systems (gutters, downpipes) - Examine anchor bolts at foundation level Every 5 years: - Professional structural assessment - Coating renewal if needed - Replace worn sealants and gaskets Common Issues and Solutions Surface rust - Humidity exposure - Spot sanding + touch-up paint Loose bolts - Vibration/wind - Re-torque to specification Panel damage - Impact or storm - Replace damaged section Coating fading - UV exposure - Re-coat every 10-15 years Quality steel structures from reputable manufacturers like Laotie Steel Structure (https://www.laotie-steel.com) require minimal maintenance when properly installed.
